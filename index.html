    Our Mission and Solution

    What We Aim To Solve

    Designing An Online Learning Platform

    A website / tool that is integrated into a service such as e-class or is a standalone tool that is created with the idea of every individual learning on their own terms and through their own learning habits.

    Optimizing The Learning Platform For Privacy

    Encryption, and access control softwares. We collect only the minimum amount of data necessary to provide the service. We avoid storing sensitive information that is not required for the core functionality of the platform.

    Providing Opportunities For University Students

    Implementing an AI-driven personalized learning and predictive analytics to highschool student could direct students to their wanted career. Using this data the student provided by the student, the AI would allow students to focus on their weak areas that are needed for the future career.
